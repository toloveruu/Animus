Information

Here is just a little bit of information explaining Animus. It's creation, and what you have to do with it.

Animus was not originally called Animus. It actually started out as a very small hangout group. For Alex and his friends, but Alex had other ideas. He realised the name he had for his discord server (amongst other things) were lacking. So he decided to rename the discord server. From there he decided to make a website for his discord server. He has plans for this discord server. He envisions lots of gamers in Animus. Able to talk to one another about games, or whatever else. He sees it as a place for people to meet new friends. Potentially even best friends. But he can't do this without people joining. That's where YOU come in!

We hope to see you in Animus soon!
